About Me

As a Business Analytics specialist with a robust foundation in Chartered Accountancy, I combine technical proficiency in tools like Python, Tableau, Power BI, and Alteryx with an analytical mindset to deliver insights that drive impactful business outcomes. My expertise lies in translating complex datasets into actionable insights that optimize business processes and inform strategic decision-making.

Currently pursuing an MSc in Business Analytics from Royal Holloway, University of London, I have developed a strong command over predictive modeling, data visualization, and process optimization. I have successfully applied these skills across multiple projects, from forecasting hospital stays for the NHS to assessing consistency in MotoGP steward decisions, focusing on both operational efficiency and stakeholder sentiment analysis.
